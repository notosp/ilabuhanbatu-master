+ General

    • a. What is iLabuhanbatu?

      iLabuhanbatu is a digital library application equipped with eReader to read eBook and social media feature, initiated by National Library of Indonesia, in cooperation with PT.Woolu Akasara Maya (Aksaramaya) as the application developer.

    • b. What is ePustaka?

      ePustaka is a digital library which contains thousands of eBook collections that can be borrowed and read online or offline through variety of operating systems and devices such as smartphone, tablet, and desktop/laptop.

    • c. What are the requirements to borrow eBook in iLabuhanbatu?

      Requirement to borrow eBook in iLabuhanbatu, you need to have:
      –An android or iOS smartphone, desktop or laptop
      –Internet
      –MRegister to become a member of ePustaka iLabuhanbatu via iLabuhanbatu application.

    • d. What are the regulations/rules to borrow eBook in iLabuhanbatu?

      Regulations/rules to borrow eBook in iLabuhanbatu are:
      –One-year membership period and can be renewed automatically
      –As long as still remain being a member of ePustaka iLabuhanbatu, free to borrow any available eBook.
      –The borrowed eBook can only be read for 7 calendar days.
      –If the eBook is unavailable due to other user is borrowing it, will be inputted into waiting list and can borrow it once the other user has met the end of borrowing period.

    • e. What is eReader?

      eeReader is an application to read eBook with ePub or PDF format developed by Aksaramaya equipped with various features to read such as:
      –Change the background color of eBook pages,
      –View table of content,
      –Change font type and kind,
      –Set font size,
      –Give bookmark,
      –Copy few words to post them to social media,atau
      –Recommend ebook to other users,
      –Find words/phrase in eBook,
      –Adjust lighting when reading the eBook.
      –Make notes

    • f. Where can I download iLabuhanbatu?

      You can download it in Play Store for Android, Appstore for iOS with keyword “iLabuhanbatu” and you can also download it via www.iLabuhanbatu.moco.co.id website for hybrid format that is accessible using PC/laptop.

    • g. How much is the price for iLabuhanbatu application?

      iLabuhanbatu is free of charge.

    • h. How much is the cost to become ePustaka member?

      To become ePustaka member in iLabuhanbatu is free of charge, not even a single rupiah will be charge, it’s free.

    • i. Why should I use iLabuhanbatu ?

       iLabuhanbatu users can make friends, interact and recommend the interesting books to each other. User can also read a variety of books in one application, which is easy, practical and fast, anytime and anywhere. Through iLabuhanbatu, user/reader can also interact with the author.

    • j. Does iLabuhanbatu have website that can be visited?

      Yes, of course. You can visit iLabuhanbatu.moco.co.id website and if you want to know the collection of books, you can also visit iLabuhanbatu.moco.co.id website

    • k. Can I borrow the books via iLabuhanbatu.moco.co.id website?

      No. You can’t borrow books from iLabuhanbatu website. You have to use iLabuhanbatu application to borrow books.

    • l. Can I read the book that I have obtained directly in iLabuhanbatu.moco.co.id website?

      No. We do not provide the feature to read books in the website. The books can only be read via iLabuhanbatu application.

    • m. What is feed?

      Feed is a collection of public information. Information included in feed is:
      –When followers add books
      –When followers join ePustaka
      –When there is any changes in User Badges Status
      –When new books are added in the followed ePustaka
      –When books are available to be borrowed

    • n. How do I change my username in iLabuhanbatu?

      Go to profile page, click pen-shaped-icon. From there you can change your username.

    • o. What is badge status?

      Badge status is user’s read-level. There are several read-level in iLabuhanbatu which are Newbie, Bookworm, and Socializer.
      If the user wants to up the level, the user shall meet several requirements.

      Newbie to Bookworm
      –Complete Profile
      –Posted > 5 Comments
      –Download 10 books to shelf
      –Open/Read 5 Books

      Bookworm to Socializer
      –Follow 10 People
      –Share 10 Times
      –Recommend 5 Books
      –Get 10 Followers

    • p. What is current, want and history?

      Current is the collections of books that are already owned. On Current, user can delete the collections of books that may not wanted to be read anymore, to delete the collections of books is really easy only by using the info button in current page.
      Want is the collections of books that are desired by user but have not got the opportunity to borrow it yet
      History is the collections of books that have finished with percentage of book finish reach 100%. On history, user can also delete collections of books that have finished.

    • q. Are the books that we have obtained can be reviewed or commented?

      Yes. If you want to review your book, you need to go to book details page, in right side of tab you will find tab labeled comment. That is where you can review or comment on other user’s review.

    • r. How do I change my password in iLabuhanbatu?

      To change your password in iLabuhanbatu, You need to go to profile menu and click pen-shaped-icon. Select/tap on password column. Then new page for edit password will be opened. In the edit password page, you will be asked to input your old password. Then input the new password and in password confirmation column, input the new password again, and click save button.

    + Getting Started

    • a. How to register in iLabuhanbatu?

      Before login to iLabuhanbatu, you need to have iLabuhanbatu account first. Do the following:
      1. Username  : Full name
      2. Email     : Email address, as your user name to Login
      3. Password : Password to login into the application
      4. Confirm Password : repeat the same password
      5. Sign up :To send your registration

    • b. How to start iLabuhanbatu?

      To start it, you need to login through login menu. Do the following:
      1. Input email address
      2. Input password
      3. Select Sign in button

    • c. How do I download books in iLabuhanbatu application?

      To obtain the books, you need to open book details page. After that, click Get Book button then options will be shown whether you want to purchase, rent, or borrow it. After you select your option and finish the transaction, the selected book will be downloaded automatically.

    • d. Apakah buku yang telah saya unduh akan hilang ketika saya keluar dari iLabuhanbatu?

      Tidak. Untuk buku yang Anda sudah miliki akan tersimpan di dalam server kami.

    • e. Will the downloaded books be gone after I log out from iLabuhanbatu?

       No. For books that you already borrowed will be stored in our server.

    • f. What method can I use to read digital books in iLabuhanbatu?

      You can borrow it.

    • g. How do I borrow eBook in iLabuhanbatu?

      When you are in book details page. Click Get Book/Borrow button. You will be asked to input the same password with password that you use to login to iLabuhanbatu. After you successfully input your password, the books that you borrow will be downloaded automatically.

    • h. How many books can I store in iLabuhanbatu?

      As many as you can borrow

    • i. How can I join ePustaka?

      To join epustaka, you can click  button. After joining, you can read all books available in ePustaka by borrowing them

    • j. How can I view notifications that I received?

       To view NOTIFICATION is quite easy. You only need to click the bell icon/symbol in main page/dashboard.

    • k. How do I find friends in iLabuhanbatu?

      Go to book details page then open “is reading” and “has read” tab. In that tab you will find other users. Please select the user in the tab by clicking the picture. After that you will go to profile page consisting user information (username, badges status, description, wishlist, follower list, following list, and reading list and also follow button). This follow button is used to follow other users.

    • l. Can I send message via iLabuhanbatu?

      Yes. You can use inbox feature; inbox is a feature to have conversation with other users. To start conversation with other user for the first time, can be perform by going to user profile of the user to have conversation with, click chat button () on the upper right side of User Profile, it will bring you to chat page.
